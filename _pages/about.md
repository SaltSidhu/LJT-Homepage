# Junteng Liu

Welcome to my personal homepage! I am a first-year PhD candidate at the [HKUST NLP Group](https://nlp.hkust.org/), Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, advised by Professor [Junxian He](https://junxianhe.com/). I graduated from [Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU)](https://www.sjtu.edu.cn/) in June 2024.

---

## Research Interests

My research focuses on **Natural Language Processing** and **Machine Learning**. I am particularly interested in:

- **LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning** - Developing methods to enhance the reasoning capabilities of large language models through reinforcement learning
- **Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLM)** - Understanding and mitigating hallucination problems in vision-language models, especially for chart understanding
- **LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability** - Exploring the truthfulness properties inside LLMs and developing interpretable frameworks for understanding model behaviors
