## C-Eval: A Multi-Level Multi-Discipline Chinese Evaluation Suite for Foundation Models

### Authors
Yuzhen Huang, Yuzhuo Bai, Zhihao Zhu, Junlei Zhang, Jinghan Zhang, Tangjun Su, Junteng Liu, Chuancheng Lv, Yikai Zhang, Jiayi Lei, Yao Fu, Maosong Sun, Junxian He

### Abstract
This paper presents C-Eval, a comprehensive multi-level multi-discipline Chinese evaluation suite for foundation models. C-Eval provides a rigorous benchmark for evaluating language models on a wide range of Chinese language understanding and generation tasks across different difficulty levels and domains.
