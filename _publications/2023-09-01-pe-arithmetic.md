## Composing Parameter-Efficient Modules with Arithmetic Operations

### Authors
Jinghan Zhang, Shiqi Chen, Junteng Liu, Junxian He

### Abstract
This paper explores composing parameter-efficient modules with arithmetic operations for more flexible and efficient model adaptation. We propose a novel framework that enables composition of different parameter-efficient modules through arithmetic operations, significantly enhancing model adaptation capabilities.
