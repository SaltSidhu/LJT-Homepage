## In-Context Sharpness as Alerts: An Inner Representation Perspective for Hallucination Mitigation

### Authors
Shiqi Chen, Miao Xiong, Junteng Liu, Zhengxuan Wu, Teng Xiao, Siyang Gao, Junxian He

### Abstract
This paper introduces In-Context Sharpness as Alerts, an inner representation perspective for hallucination mitigation in large language models. We propose a novel approach to detect and mitigate hallucinations by analyzing the sharpness of in-context representations.
