## On the Perception Bottleneck of VLMs for Chart Understanding

### Authors
Junteng Liu, Weihao Zeng, Xiwen Zhang, Yijun Wang, Zifei Shan, Junxian He

### Abstract
This paper investigates the perception bottleneck of Vision-Language Models (VLMs) when understanding charts and visual data. We identify key challenges in how VLMs process visual information and propose novel approaches to improve chart understanding capabilities.
