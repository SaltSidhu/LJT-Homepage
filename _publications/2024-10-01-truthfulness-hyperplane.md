## On the Universal Truthfulness Hyperplane Inside LLMs

### Authors
Junteng Liu, Shiqi Chen, Yu Cheng, Junxian He

### Abstract
This paper investigates the universal truthfulness hyperplane inside large language models, providing insights into model behaviors and interpretability. We identify a universal direction in the embedding space that correlates with model truthfulness across different tasks and architectures.
